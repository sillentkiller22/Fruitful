Fruitful Android Mobile Application Module:
1.GPS system for tractors and labour by android mobile.
2.Mobile app can be avalable in hindi and english language.
3.Both online and offline payment procedure for rent.
4.All details of tractor owner and labour by registration.
5.Feedback and review service for farmers reference.
6.On the basis of review labours and tractors get ranking.
7.Facility of taking photo of initial and final reading of tractor meter.
8.We put the general information about crops for sowing.
9.All the tractor accessories,seed and fertilizers are avalabile online with price.
10.We have tractor owner and labour phone number for calling.

	Our main aim to empower the farmers,tractor owner and lobour lifestyle

the android phone.The mobile avaliable in regional language for farmer like
hind and english.We can put payment procedure in our application.For the rent

It leads to proof our tractor rent.We put the general information about various
